About SmartSecure AI

An intelligent insurance fraud detection system designed to help insurers and claims adjusters identify suspicious claims quickly and accurately.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce insurance fraud losses by leveraging cutting-edge machine learning to analyze claim patterns, detect anomalies, and flag high-risk cases for investigation — all in real time.

Our goal is to protect insurers while ensuring legitimate claims are processed faster, creating a fair and efficient insurance ecosystem.

How It Works

Our system uses a trained Random Forest model that analyzes 8 key claim features to determine fraud probability:

  • Months as Customer — Tenure with the insurer indicates relationship stability
  • Age — Policyholder age helps identify demographic patterns
  • Policy Annual Premium — Yearly premium amount for coverage assessment
  • Umbrella Limit — Additional coverage limit indicates policy scope
  • Total Claim Amount — Overall claim value for ratio analysis
  • Injury, Property & Vehicle Claims — Breakdown by claim type reveals patterns

The model outputs a fraud probability score (0–100%). Claims above 70% are flagged as high risk and recommended for immediate investigation.
